Plastic Cleaning Drive

Our Mission

We are a nonprofit organization dedicated to reducing plastic pollution and cleaning up the environment. Our mission is to educate the public about the impacts of plastic waste and to organize community clean-up events.

Our Programs

We offer a range of programs and initiatives to support our mission, including:

  • Beach clean-ups
  • River clean-ups
  • Plastic waste education workshops
  • Partnerships with local businesses to reduce plastic use
